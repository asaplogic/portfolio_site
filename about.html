Hello!

I’m a self taught digital product designer with a degree in Industrial Design currently based in Chicago. Ever since I dipped my toes into web development and created my first website I have fallen in love with code, taking classes in everything from C++ and Python to HTML, CSS always trying to grow my knowledge and ability.

I have a strong belief that good design and engineering can and should work hand in hand to create a great product for the end user. I pride myself on not only being able to speak to the backend of a platform but also the end user.

When i’m not creating you can often find me cycling, running, or getting lost outdoors with my dog.
